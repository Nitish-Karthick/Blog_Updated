Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. It encompasses a broad set of practices, tools, and concepts designed to safeguard digital assets and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information. As organizations and individuals rely more on digital systems, the importance of cybersecurity has grown exponentially.
